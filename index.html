We invest with a mission to contribute to the transition to a sustainable world

Climate change is something we have to include in every single analysis, every investment. Most people think—or hope—that global warming is something their children or grandchildren will have to reckon with. Climate change however is happening suddenly and soon. The world is skidding toward a future that’s overheated and underwater—and where carbon will be treated as a costly waste product that needs to be captured and stored

About us

CLMT Partners invests in opportunities where profit, purpose and long-term impact are aligned. Our founders have extensive experience in investing, entrepreneurship, impact ventures and managing companies across the full life cycle from startup to (public market) exit. We partner with visionary business leaders who champion sustainability and we act as a catalyst of private capital participation in the transition to a sustainable world. CLMT Partners was founded in 2019 and is based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands

Approach

We invest globally with strong focus and conviction in the energy transition and plant based food. We bring the financial benefits of sustainability to private capital and provide business leaders an alternative source of financing. We seek for additionality and for our investments to have a positive impact on the world. We work with a circle of investors and partners who believe that extraordinary long-term returns can be achieved when profit and purpose are aligned. We are a by invitation, members only investment community. Our members are family offices, high net worth individuals and institutions.

Mission

Our mission is to act as a catalyst of private capital participation in the transition to a sustainable world. The world is at crossroads as tackling climate change will affect everything from transportation, real estate, manufacturing and agriculture to energy production and the design of cities. Our investments embrace sustainability and positive impact as a competitive advantage. We don’t compromise profit for purpose. We believe every investment is an opportunity to champion long term sustainability.
